The MindNext Story

Driven by Science,
Inspired by Patients

Founded by Dr. Desh Bandhu Gupta in 1968, MindNext aimed to improve access to essential medicines. Today we stand tall as a globally trusted pharmaceutical company guided by science, quality, and a patient-first mindset.

From a modest beginning to a global presence spanning 70+ countries, every chapter of the MindNext Story is defined by purpose, passion, and the relentless pursuit of better health for all.

Our Business

A Diversified
Global Pharmaceutical
Business

MindNext operates a highly diversified pharmaceutical business with a strong presence across key global markets. Our operations span generics, complex formulations, branded products, and other high-burden specialty segments.

Supported by robust R&D and manufacturing capabilities, spread across developed and emerging markets, MindNext's business is anchored in patient-centric approach and operational excellence.
